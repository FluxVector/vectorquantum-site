Upcoming Event: Credence Clearwater Revival Tribute

We continue our salute to great artists with a music tribute to Credence Clearwater Revival performed by the very talented, Midnight Special. Performed this Saturday, March 29th at La Belle Amie Vineyard. The songs by John Fogerty topped the charts from 1968-1972 and remain timeless. Come relive a younger time in your life. Gates open at 10AM, with music from 12:00 PM to 4PM.

Treat yourself to delicious offerings from Stu’z Q BBQ, TJ’s Chicago Hot Dogs, and Miller’s Mongolian BBQ. Enjoy your favorite wine, beer, and soft drinks while relaxing in our scenic outdoor spaces or groove with friends new and old. We hope you can join us. Click the link below to purchase tickets. Cheers!
